# Organization Health & Integrity

## Scoring
- **Integrity Score**: Starts at 100%. Penalized by broken dependencies (Critical Errors) and missing non-critical structure (Warnings).
- **CEO Visibility**: Integrated directly into the Executive Workspace to highlight organizational debt.

## Checks Performed
- Missing mandatory entities.
- Broken foreign keys (orphan positions, teams, etc).
- Circular references in position hierarchy.
