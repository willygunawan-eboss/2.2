# Organization Tree & Dependency Graph

## Overview
The organizational tree is a directed acyclic graph mapping the structural flow from Company -> Branch -> Division -> Department -> Section -> Team.

Positions are mapped to their respective Departments, serving as the leaf nodes in this structural tree, but can also have their own parent-child relationships (Hierarchy).

## Dependency Graph
The `DependencyGraph` component visually explains the organizational levels defined by the ERP system, ensuring that stakeholders understand the rigid classification hierarchy used for structural reporting.
