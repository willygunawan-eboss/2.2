# Employee Workspace

## Overview
Employee Workspace is the central hub for all activities related to an Employee within the ICHANGEBOSS ERP V2. It moves away from traditional CRUD forms into a cohesive, Master-Detail environment where everything about an Employee—their organization assignment, employment history, contacts, documents, and audit trail—can be viewed and managed from a single unified view.

## Philosophy
- **Workspace is the Center:** It acts as the standard template for future workspaces (Customer, Vendor, Asset, Project).
- **Master-Detail Layout:** Left panel handles exploration and filtering, right panel handles detailed tabbed workspaces.
- **Zero-Context Switching:** All information is accessible without navigating to a different page.
